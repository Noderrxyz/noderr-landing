## What is the ATE (Autonomous Trading Engine)?

The ATE is NOT just an execution layer - it's a **sophisticated strategy mutation and validation system** that uses evolutionary algorithms and machine learning to continuously improve trading strategies.

### 3. Strategy Mutation Process

The ATE doesn't just execute strategies - it **mutates and evolves them**:

1. **Strategy Genome Representation** - Each strategy is encoded as a "genome"
2. **Evolutionary Optimization** - Strategies are mutated using genetic algorithms
3. **Multi-Stage Validation** - Only strategies that pass rigorous testing are deployed
4. **Continuous Adaptation** - Online learning allows strategies to adapt to changing markets
